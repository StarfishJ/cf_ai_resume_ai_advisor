A day in the life
(*Real innovation rarely follows a perfect schedule, but here's a glimpse!)

9:00 AM - Arrive at the office. First stop: Our micro-kitchen for coffee and breakfast snacks. Join other early (and not-so-early) birds as you fuel up for the day ahead.

9:30 AM - Daily team stand-up. Share updates on your project, whether it's distributed training systems or chip design. Your mentor helps navigate any unfamiliar territory.

10:00 AM - Deep work time. Dive into your project - optimizing ML frameworks, developing firmware, or designing next-gen AI accelerator chips.

12:00 PM - Intern cohort lunch. Compare notes with fellow interns and learn about the different technical challenges across teams.

1:30 PM - Collaboration with your mentor. Debug issues, brainstorm solutions, and ask those questions that have been on your mind.

3:00 PM - Team meeting. See how your work connects to Annapurna Labs' broader mission of revolutionizing cloud computing.

4:00 PM - Back to development. Continue project work, testing, or documentation.

5:30 PM - Wrap up your day, having contributed to systems that will impact millions of AWS customers.

6:00 PM - (Optional) Social time with the intern cohort. Pizza, bowling, tech talks, etc. There's usually something happening!

Remember: While each day brings different challenges, you'll always be doing meaningful work that pushes the boundaries of AI acceleration, supported by a team that's as passionate about innovation as you are.
